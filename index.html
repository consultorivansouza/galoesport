Club Information

Foundation

Atlético Mineiro was founded in 1908 in Belo Horizonte, Brazil. Since its foundation, the club has built a strong identity and tradition in Brazilian football.

Stadium

The team plays at Arena MRV, one of the most modern stadiums in Brazil, known for its passionate atmosphere and strong connection with the fans.

Current Moment (SAF)

Atlético Mineiro is currently managed as a SAF (Sociedade Anônima do Futebol), which has improved financial organization and strengthened the club's future.

Curiosities

The club mascot is a rooster, known as "Galo". Atlético has one of the most passionate fan bases in Brazil and is famous for historic comebacks and unforgettable matches.
